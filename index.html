APPLICATION AND DOCUMENTATION

Application for your EU residence document can be made by following the steps below:
1. print out and complete the OD1 application form (which can be found on the tables and forms page)
2. prepare to be able to submit the following to the State Administration:
  •   documentation for your basis for residence (find out more about documentation for your basis for
residence below)
  •   your original passport and/or your national ID card
  •   an accurate passport-size photo (two if you are not an EU/EEA national). See photo requirements
here. 
3. attend in person at the State Administration to submit the above application form.

CIVIL REGISTRATION NUMBER

In order to get a CPR number you must meet these criteria:
1. You are staying in Denmark for more than 3 months.
2. You have a registration certificate, if you are an EU citizen.
3. You hold a residence permit, if you are a non-EU citizen.
4. You have a place to live.
With the conditions fulfilled, you can visit your local kommune and ask for a civil registration number.

NEMID CODE CARD

To get a NEMID code card you can do the following things once you have your CPR:
1. Get NemID via citizen services.
2. Get NemID via online banking.
3. Order NemID online (requires Danish passport or Danish Driver's licence).
